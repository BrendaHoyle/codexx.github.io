What does chadev mean?

Chadev is short for Chattanooga developer.

What's our purpose?

Our goal is to create a central location for Chattanooga developers, programmers, and hackers to ask questions, show off projects, share links, post events, etc.

How can I get involved?

We are still working out the details. For now, we have created a subreddit that will work as the hub. We will let you know along the way what other shapes this project might take.
